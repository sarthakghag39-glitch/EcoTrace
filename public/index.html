Why SDG 13: Climate Action Matters

Climate change is a global emergency that threatens every aspect of human life. Greenhouse gas emissions from human activities are driving global temperature rise, leading to rising sea levels, severe droughts, and devastating wildfires.

The Problem: Average global temperatures have increased by 1.1°C compared to pre-industrial times. Without rapid and systemic reductions in CO₂ emissions, warming could exceed the critical threshold of 1.5°C by 2030, leading to irreversible tipping points.

Our Solution: By tracking your personal carbon footprint, you become conscious of your impact. EcoTrace provides a micro-accounting of emissions, helping you make actionable adjustments to your transit, diet, and energy usage.
